Generative Art.

Generative Art refers to any art practice where the artist uses a system, such as a set of natural language rules a computer program, a machine, or other procedural invention, which is then set into motion with some degree of autonomy to or resulting in a complex work of art. Please read Philip Galanter's research paper for more details on Generative Art & Creative coding.
