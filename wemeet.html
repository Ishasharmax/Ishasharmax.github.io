WeMeet is a socializing app that strives to help people come together and have a great time enjoying local cultural activities.







DURATION

August - September (3 weeks)

ROLE

UX/UI Designer

RESPONSIBILITIES

User Research, Personas, Empathy Map, Affinity Map, User Journey Map, Style Guide, User Flow, Wireframes, Lo-fi and Hi-fi Prototypes, Usability Testing, Logo

TOOLS

Figma, Photoshop

PROBLEMS

- Language barrier
- Hard to find people to hangout with due to busy schedule.
- Hard to find new venues to socialize.
- Hard to maintain friendships.
- Not having enough information, in an organized way, about local activities/events happening around.
- Not having offline access to map and event information.
- Not coming across budget friendly activities.
- Lots of activities to choose from with no proper filtering options.
- Can’t find genuine reviews.


GOALS

- Enjoy local activities with new people.
- Minimize research time.
- Share unforgettable experiences.
- Connect with new people & establish long term connections with them.
- Find people to hangout with after work or during the travel.
- Learn about new cultures & indulge in local activities.
- Be able to find activities in the language of choice.
- Be able to find activities of your choice.
- Want to view & give out genuine & valuable reviews.

LESSONS LEARNED

This project was extremely fun to create. From the user research to designing the prototypes, I’ve got to learn a lot about the perspectives of different users. Users are majorily looking for an straightforward app that would enable them to meet and go on social activities together. This app is ideal for anyone who has recently moved to a new place or is traveling around and is in look for social activities.

I think creating an app from the start to the beginning, touching all the variables, and finally creating a coherent product was in itself a challenge, but to be able to somehow condense it and create an easy to use app for the users was the major challenge.

This also made me realize that creating a well-functioning app is not an easy job, as it required a lot of iteration to be able to get to the product our users desire, but in all this was a great product to create.

NEXT STEPS

I would love to iterate and refine the designs created after getting the second round of usability testing from more users. Due to limited perspectives and limited user testing, I might have not been able to get much more valuable feedback necessary for this app to be a successful one. After making those revisions to the screens and prototype, I would continue to add some more features required by the users and the again conduct user testing for the feedback. Another thing, I would want to change is the consistency of the icons I’ve used, they don’t all seem to go together with one another at the moment.

The app is heading on the right direction and finding any way to make further improvements to the process and experience would only make it stronger in the end.
