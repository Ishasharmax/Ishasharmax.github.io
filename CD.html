CASHDROP is a mobile commerce platform that helps businesses create and manage an online storefront from a smartphone. I created a conceptual website UI for the company in a Desktop layout.







ROLE

UX/UI Designer

RESPONSIBILITIES

Personas, Wireframing, Mockups, Design Principles, Market Research

TOOLS

Adobe XD, Figma, Photoshop, After Effects, Xtensio

SUMMARY

The brief was to redesign the company’s website such that it incorporates the branding properly and enhances the overall experience of the site for the end user.

  • Validate user personas from the brief and research.
  • Rebuild the Information Architecture (IA) for the website to meet the needs of three key user personas.
  • Make sure that the redesigned website is in line with the brand’s identity and guidelines.
