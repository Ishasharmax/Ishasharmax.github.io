Musist is a app design concept for local musicians in the town, who are in look for best and affordable music event venues, instruments, add on musicians, etc..







Year

2021

Role

Designer

Responsibilities

Development of Personas, Wireframing, Mockups, User Research, Design Principles, Market Research, Typography, Illustration

Tools

Adobe Xd, Figma

Project Summary

Musist helps local musicians with a convenient way to search for the best and most affordable venues, instruments, add on musicians around the town.

Problem Statement

Provide grocery store customers with a convenient way to maneuver around the store to find the items they are looking for efficiently and effectively.

Audience

The audience for this app would ideally be musicians and people who are passionate about giving the best and affordable music experiences to their audiences. This app could be utilized both by younger and older audiences who are familiar and reliant on technology for recreational activities. This app can play a beneficial role in the life of people, who are in need for quick venues, instruments, or add on musicians for their gigs.

Pain Points

  • 1. Too many options to choose from, whether musicians, venues, or instruments, which can interfere with users' availability of time.
  • 2. Lack of concise resources for music events, which makes it harder for music players and lovers to find right type of gig and venue for them.
